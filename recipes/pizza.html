Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon yeast
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoon coconut sugar
  • 6 cups '00' flour, or sub AP or bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon olive oil, extra virgin

Steps

  1. Add the yeast, water and sugar to a large mixing bowl. Stir to dissolve yeast and sugar. Let sit for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add the flour, salt and EVOO. Knead in the bowl for about five minutes.
  3. Cover the bowl and let rise for 1-2 hours.
  4. If you cannot use the dough right away, roll into a ball and place in plastic wrap - place in the fridge. When ready to use the dough, simply roll it out to a thin state and add toppings - bake and serve. Bake time for one large pizza runs 16-22 minutes at 450 degrees.
